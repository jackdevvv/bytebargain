Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

1. Introduction

ByteBargains ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the ByteBargains website and mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process personal information in connection with our website, mobile applications, and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, password, and profile information.
  • Order Information: When you make a purchase, we collect billing address, shipping address, payment method details, and order history.
  • Communications: We collect information when you contact our customer service team, participate in surveys, or subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Payment Information: We collect credit card numbers and other payment information, which are encrypted and processed securely.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We collect information about the devices you use to access our services, including IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about your interactions with our website and applications, including pages visited, products viewed, search queries, and time spent on pages.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather analytics.
  • Location Information: We may collect approximate location information based on IP address for shipping and personalization purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders and transactions
  • Providing customer support and responding to your inquiries
  • Sending transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates, etc.)
  • Personalizing your shopping experience and product recommendations
  • Conducting marketing and promotional activities (with your consent)
  • Analyzing and improving our website, products, and services
  • Preventing fraud and enhancing security
  • Complying with legal obligations and resolving disputes

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us with:

  • Payment processing and fraud prevention
  • Shipping and logistics
  • Customer service and support
  • Marketing and analytics
  • Website hosting and maintenance

4.2 Legal Requirements and Business Transfers

We may disclose your information when required by law, court order, or governmental request. Additionally, if ByteBargains is involved in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4.3 Your Consent

We will share your information with third parties only with your explicit consent or as described in this Privacy Policy.

5. Security of Your Information

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure payment processing
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Access restrictions and authentication protocols
  • Employee training on data protection

However, no security measure is 100% effective. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to access, review, and update your personal information. You can manage your account settings or contact us for assistance.

6.2 Marketing Communications

You can opt-out of marketing emails by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any promotional email or managing your preferences in your account settings.

6.3 Cookies and Tracking

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, although this may limit certain website functionality.

6.4 Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and personal information by contacting our Privacy Team. We will comply with applicable legal requirements.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete such information.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of the sale of your personal information. Contact us to exercise these rights.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our services constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
